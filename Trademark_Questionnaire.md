## 9. Business Documents Required

- **Individual:** N/A  
- **Proprietorship:** GST / Trade License  
- **Partnership Firm:** Partnership Deed + GST  
- **LLP:** LLP Incorporation Certificate + LLP Deed + GST  
- **Private Limited / OPC:** Company Incorporation Certificate + GST  
- **Trust:** Trust Deed + Trust Registration Certificate  
- **Society:** Society Registration Certificate  

*Attach MSME (UDYOG AADHAAR) Certificate if available.*

---

## 10. Government Fees for Trademark Application

1. Individual / Proprietorship Firm: **Rs. 4,500 per class per mark**  
2. Others (with MSME Certificate): **Rs. 4,500 per class per mark**  
3. Others (without MSME Certificate): **Rs. 9,000 per class per mark**

---

## Important Notes

- If the brand name is already in use, a **notarized User Affidavit** on Rs. 100 stamp paper is mandatory.
- Amazon accepts only **Wordmark** trademarks.
- If your brand includes a logo, you may need **two applications**:
  - Device Mark (with logo)
  - Wordmark (without logo)
